Privacy Policy for Maxi Sales

Last updated: April 21, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes Our policies and procedures on the collection, use and disclosure of Your information when You use the Service and tells You about Your privacy rights.

1. Personal Data Collection

While using Our Service, We may ask You to provide personally identifiable information such as your Email, Full Name, and Phone Number.

Camera Access & Usage

Our Application requires access to your device's Camera to provide the following features:

  • Barcode Scanning: To allow users to quickly search for products and identify items by scanning their barcodes.
  • Product Identification: To capture or process images for identifying specific sales items within the inventory.

We do not store photos or videos from your camera on our servers unless explicitly required for a specific business transaction initiated by you.

Background Location

We collect Location Data to support field sales operations:

  • To enable tracking of sales routes and attendance monitoring even when the app is closed or not in use.
  • This feature is only active when the "GPS Tracking" mode is enabled by the user.

Customer Data (Third-Party Information)

The Application allows users to register customer details including:

  • Identity: Name and Business name.
  • Contact: Phone and Email.
  • Location: GPS coordinates and addresses of customer shops for delivery and visit management.

2. Use of Data

The Company uses the data to:

  • Maintain the Service and manage orders.
  • Optimize delivery routes and generate visit reports.
  • Provide technical support and improve app functionality.

3. Data Retention & Security

We retain your data only for as long as necessary for business purposes. We use standard security measures to protect your information, though no system is 100% secure.
